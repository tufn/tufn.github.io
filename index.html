Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built from the ground up for focus and clarity. No bloat, no distractions, no subscriptions.

01

Smart Notes

Rich text and Markdown editing with tags, folder organization, and instant full-text search. Your thoughts, perfectly captured and always findable.

02

Task Management

Priority levels, due dates, and smart filtering. Views that adapt to how you actually work — inbox, today, upcoming, or project-focused.

03

Digital Canvas

Freehand drawing, shapes, arrows, and text. Brainstorm visually, annotate notes, sketch wireframes. Export to PNG or SVG.

04

Calendar

Month and week views with full event management, recurring events, and reminders. Synced across all your devices so you never miss a deadline.

05

Cloud Sync

End-to-end encrypted, offline-first architecture. Your data is stored locally first and synced securely in the background. Always accessible, always private.

06

Focus Mode

One-click distraction-free mode with a built-in Pomodoro timer and Do Not Disturb. Deep work, made simple. Your flow, protected.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Tufn is a free, minimalist all-in-one productivity app that combines notes, tasks, a digital canvas, and a calendar in a single clean interface built for deep work and sustained focus. No subscriptions, no paywalls.

Tufn is currently 75% complete and targeting a Q1 2027 public release. Join the waitlist to be first in line — you'll get an email the moment we go live.

Most productivity tools suffer from feature bloat — they try to do everything and end up doing nothing particularly well. Tufn is built around a single conviction: the best tool is one that gets out of your way. Minimal interface, maximal focus. Completely free, forever.

Yes — completely, genuinely free. No free tier with a "Pro" upsell, no credit card required, no ads, no data selling. Productivity tools should be accessible to everyone. Free on every platform, forever.

Tufn will launch on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android. There will also be a web version that runs in any modern browser — no installation required.

Your notes, tasks, and data are stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is opt-in and uses end-to-end encryption — your data is encrypted before it ever leaves your device. We literally cannot read it. See our Privacy Policy.
