Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tufn?

Tufn is a minimalist all-in-one productivity app that combines notes, tasks, digital canvas, and calendar in one elegant interface designed for maximum focus.

When will Tufn launch?

Tufn is currently in development with 63% progress and estimated to launch in 2027. Join our waitlist to get early access.

What makes Tufn different?

Unlike other productivity tools, Tufn offers a truly minimalist interface with integrated digital canvas for brainstorming, all while maintaining focus on essential features.

Is Tufn free?

Tufn will offer a free version with core features and optional premium upgrades for advanced functionality.

What platforms will Tufn support?

Tufn will be available on Web, iOS, and Android with full cloud synchronization across all devices.

Can I try Tufn now?

While in development, you can join our waitlist to get updates and be among the first to try Tufn when it launches.
