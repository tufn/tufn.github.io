Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tufn?

Tufn is a minimalist all-in-one productivity app that combines notes, tasks, digital canvas, and calendar in one elegant interface designed for maximum focus.

When will Tufn launch?

Tufn is currently in development with 63% progress and estimated to launch in Q1 2027. Join our waitlist to get early access and stay updated on our progress.

What makes Tufn different?

Unlike other productivity tools, Tufn offers a truly minimalist interface with integrated digital canvas for brainstorming, all while maintaining focus on essential features without bloat.

Is Tufn free?

Tufn is completely free. We believe productivity tools should be accessible to everyone.

Which platforms will be supported?

Tufn will be available on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android. We'll also offer a web version that works in any modern browser.

Will my data be secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard encryption and never sell your data to third parties.
