Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026. We keep this simple because there isn't much to say — we collect almost nothing.

TL;DR: Tufn collects zero personal data about you as a user. The only exceptions are: (1) the email address you voluntarily provide when joining our waitlist, and (2) anonymous, non-identifiable crash reports and performance metrics that help us fix bugs. That's it.

1. Who We Are

Tufn is a minimalist productivity application currently in development, created and operated as an independent project. You can reach us at tufn-off@proton.me.

2. What We Collect — and What We Don't

We built Tufn with privacy as a first principle. Here is a complete, honest breakdown of every category of data:

Data Type Collected? Purpose
Your notes, tasks, canvas drawings, calendar events Never Stored only on your device. We have no access.
Your name, profile, or personal identifiers Never We don't ask for this and don't want it.
Browsing history, tracking pixels, cookies Never We run no analytics trackers, ad networks, or third-party tracking.
IP address or precise location Never stored May pass through our servers transiently (like any web request) but is never logged or retained.
Waitlist email address Optional, voluntary Only to notify you at launch. Never sold or shared.
Anonymous crash reports Anonymous only Stack traces and error codes with no personal data attached. Used only to fix bugs.
Anonymous performance metrics Anonymous only Aggregate load times and feature usage counts. No user IDs, no personal data.

3. Waitlist Data

When you join our waitlist, you provide your email address. This is stored securely in our database (hosted on Supabase with encryption at rest and in transit). We use this email exclusively to:

  • Notify you when Tufn launches
  • Send occasional development updates, if you opted in

We will never sell your email, share it with advertisers, add it to third-party lists, or use it for any purpose other than what is stated above. You can request deletion of your email at any time by writing to us at tufn-off@proton.me.

4. Crash Reports & Performance Metrics

To improve stability and performance, the app may collect anonymous technical data when something goes wrong or when we need to measure performance. This data is:

  • Fully anonymous — no user ID, email, or any personally identifying information is attached
  • Technical only — limited to error messages, stack traces, and timing data
  • Never linked to you — we have no way to associate this data with any individual
  • Opt-out available — you can disable this in the app settings (once the app launches)

Examples of what crash reports contain: "NullPointerException at line 142 of canvas.js". Examples of what they do not contain: your name, your notes, your tasks, or anything about you personally.

5. Your App Data Stays on Your Device

All notes, tasks, canvas drawings, and calendar data you create in Tufn are stored locally on your device. When cloud sync is enabled (an opt-in feature), your data is encrypted before leaving your device using industry-standard end-to-end encryption. We cannot read your data, and we will not try to.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following minimal third-party infrastructure:

  • Supabase — for waitlist storage only. Subject to Supabase's privacy policy.
  • Font Awesome — icon library loaded via CDN (Cloudflare). No tracking.
  • Google Fonts — for typography. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, Mixpanel, or any other behavioral tracking or analytics tool.

7. Cookies

This website does not use any tracking cookies. We use localStorage only to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode) and whether you've already joined the waitlist. This data never leaves your browser.

8. Children's Privacy

Tufn is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their email address, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we hold about you (just your email, if you joined the waitlist)
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Opt out of development update emails
  • Opt out of anonymous crash/performance reporting (in-app setting, post-launch)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at tufn-off@proton.me. We'll respond within 7 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy in a meaningful way, we will update the date at the top of this page and notify waitlist subscribers. We don't anticipate needing to collect more data — this policy reflects our core values, not just legal compliance.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests:
