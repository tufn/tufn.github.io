Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. We keep this simple because there isn't much to say — we collect almost nothing.

TL;DR: Tufn collects zero personal data about you as a user. The only exceptions are the email you optionally provide to download the app, and anonymous bug reports which may include your OS — solely to help us fix bugs.

1. Who We Are

Tufn is a minimalist productivity application, created and operated as an independent project. You can reach us at tufn-off@proton.me.

2. What We Collect

Data Type Collected? Purpose
Your notes, tasks, canvas, calendar Never Stored only on your device. We have no access.
Name, profile, personal identifiers Never We don't ask for this and don't want it.
Tracking cookies, analytics pixels Never No trackers, no ad networks, no third-party tracking.
IP address or precise location Never stored Passes through servers transiently but is never logged.
Email address Optional only Only if you choose to provide it when downloading. Never shared.
Operating system (user agent) Optional only Detected from your browser only if you submit a bug report. Not tracked otherwise.
Anonymous crash reports Anonymous only No personal data attached. Used only to fix bugs.

3. Bug Reports & Operating System Detection

When you voluntarily submit a bug report through our website, we read your browser's user agent string to detect your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux). This helps us reproduce and fix bugs on the correct platform.

We do not store the full user agent string — only the OS name (e.g. "windows"). This data is not linked to any personal identifier, is not used for tracking, and is never shared with third parties. Bug reports are entirely optional: if you don't submit one, nothing is collected.

4. Your App Data

All notes, tasks, canvas, and calendar data are stored locally on your device. When cloud sync is enabled (opt-in), your data is end-to-end encrypted before leaving your device. We cannot read your data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts for typography. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, Mixpanel, or any behavioral tracking service.

6. Cookies

This website uses no tracking cookies. We use localStorage only to remember your theme preference. This data never leaves your browser.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to know what data we hold about you, request deletion at any time, and opt out of crash/performance reporting. Email tufn-off@proton.me and we'll respond within 7 days.

8. Changes

If we change this policy meaningfully, we'll update the date above.
